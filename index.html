Welcome to Arbor Learning Center, the Homeschool Preschool


A truly new, innovative and affordable concept in homeschool preschool education.


We offer the finest curriculum available for children ages one year to school-aged. Our program is designed to prepare them for their all-important school years ahead. We are a best practices school which means that we take the best from each teaching method such as Montessori, Reggio Emilia, Waldorf, and Nature Play. We also do some traditional teaching methods as well. Rest assured whatever we do in our Preschool Sessions, Camps, Mother Morning Outs or Parent Nights Out, your child will be having fun and learning in a variety of ways.





Programs

We offer programs for children ranging from toddler to school age. If you have any questions about these programs, please feel free to contact us.



Preschoolers

At this age, children are meeting new challenges as fast as they come. Crawling, walking and eventually running are levels they accomplish in what seems like a blink of an eye. Children learn best if they are in a stimulating environment. Music, stories, outdoor play, and learning simplicities envelop much of the day. We are dedicated in helping each child develop these skills through a trusting and loving relationship.



School-Age

As children get older and more independent, they need new challenges. That’s why we offer a school-age program for before and after school care and enrichment programs that address the changing and more varied needs of growing school-age children. Reading, math, and science activities ensure that your child's mind is engaged and help create a lifelong love of learning and exploration. We also offer track out camps for school age kids.
